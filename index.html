About the Club

Our History

Established in 1942, Goldenacre Bowling Club has a rich history of fostering community spirit and promoting the sport of lawn bowls.

Our Green

Enjoy our fantastic outdoor green, ideal for both competitive league matches and relaxing social roll-ups.

The Community

We welcome players of all ages and skill levels. Join us for weekly events, social games, and local competitions.
